Netflix Audience Statistical Analysis

Comprehensive statistical analysis of Netflix viewer data to inform strategic content decisions and optimize audience engagement

Completed: December 2023
Sample Size: 179 Viewers
Tools: SPSS, Excel, Chart.js
Back to All Projects

Project Overview

This comprehensive statistical analysis examines Netflix audience data to inform strategic decisions for content releases. As competition escalates in the streaming sector, data-driven decision-making has become crucial for enhancing audience engagement, pinpointing successful genres, and refining market strategy.

The study employs multiple quantitative approaches including descriptive statistics, probability calculations, hypothesis testing, and decision-tree modeling to elucidate patterns in viewer happiness, demographic inclinations, and anticipated business results.

Key Business Impact

Identified optimal content strategy with $85,000 Expected Monetary Value, revealing that delaying launch for additional research maximizes returns compared to immediate launch alternatives.
